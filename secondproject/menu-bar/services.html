Our Services

Inspired by leading platforms like Amazon, Instacart, and DoorDash, we offer powerful tools to help African businesses thrive both in the U.S. and internationally. Through our two divisions — KoraRush for e-commerce and AconExpress for logistics — ACON Group delivers seamless, scalable solutions.

KoraRush

Our e-commerce platform connects African grocery and retail stores with online shoppers. We provide website storefronts, online ordering, payment integration, and delivery logistics — all tailored for the African diaspora.

Explore KoraRush

AconExpress

Our logistics division handles cargo shipping, last-mile delivery, and international freight forwarding. From barrels to bulk shipments, we make it easy for African businesses and families to connect globally.
