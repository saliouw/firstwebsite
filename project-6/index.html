Learn About Solar Energy

What Is Solar Energy?

Solar energy is power harnessed from the sun using photovoltaic (PV) panels. It's a clean, renewable source that can power homes, businesses, and even portable devices without relying on fossil fuels.

How Much Can You Save With Solar?

Depending on your location and energy usage, switching to solar can save you between $10,000 to $30,000 over the system’s lifetime. Solar users also enjoy protection from rising utility rates.

Solar Tax Incentives in the U.S.

Thanks to the federal solar tax credit (ITC), you can deduct up to 30% of your solar installation costs from your federal taxes. Many states offer additional rebates and net metering benefits.

Is Solar Right for Renters?

Renters can benefit from solar too! Portable solar generators, battery kits, and balcony solar panels make it easy to enjoy clean energy without owning a home. Products from EcoFlow and GoSun are renter-friendly options.
