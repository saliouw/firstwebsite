About KoraRush

Bringing African grocery stores online and delivering to homes across the U.S.

Who We Are

KoraRush is a community-driven delivery service that partners with African stores to make culturally essential groceries available at your doorstep. We empower local businesses with digital tools and a modern logistics platform.

Our Mission

To connect African grocery stores to the world through technology, accessibility, and trust.

Our Vision

To be the leading African grocery delivery platform in the diaspora, bridging tradition and innovation.
