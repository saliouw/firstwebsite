Our Logistics Services

ACON Logistics specializes in international freight forwarding and shipping solutions designed for families, small businesses, and exporters connecting the U.S. with West Africa.

Car Shipping

We offer safe and cost-effective vehicle shipping from the U.S. to West Africa. Whether you’re sending a single car or managing bulk exports, ACON handles the documentation, loading, and tracking.

Barrels & Household Goods

Ship personal items, barrels, and home supplies to family or business partners. Our network ensures fast customs clearance and on-time delivery at major West African ports.

Local Business Support

We partner with U.S. and African businesses to handle logistics, inventory movement, and outsourced shipping needs. Let us simplify your regional and international supply chain.

Air & Ocean Freight

Choose between fast air freight or budget-friendly ocean freight for your cargo. ACON Logistics manages each step—from pickup to customs to final delivery—with care and efficiency.
